Online auction information

This is online auction page. You can sell and buy items.

Incorrect auction will be deleted. Users with inappropriate actions will be banned.

For more information, please contact us via email: auckionaiinfo@gmail.com

Selling item

To sell item you must create an auction. There is some restrictions in auction create procedure.

- Auction leanght cannot exceed 30 days.

- Creating auction must include at least one photo of item.

- All information must be filled in creation form.

Payment information

User can buy pay for auction with Paypal account or Credit Card.

For auction creator funds will be transfered to his selected payout form (Paypal account or Bank account)

Funds for auction will be transfered to auction owner on seccessful payment from auction winner.

Auctino winner has 7 days to pay. After that payment will not be eligible.
